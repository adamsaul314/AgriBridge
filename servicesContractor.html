Services for Agricultural Contractors

Tailored Staffing Solutions for Your Farm in New Zealand

Staffing Solutions

At Agribridge, we pride ourselves on sourcing the best quality staff for your upcoming harvest. We select candidates from a range of agricultural colleges around Ireland and accept applications from young farmers. Our recruitment process includes vetting, interviewing, and checking references to ensure that each candidate is perfectly suited to your needs.

Available Roles: We specialize in filling roles such as farm hands, machinery operators, and farm managers, providing you with the skilled workers you need for your operations.

Customizable Workforce for Your Farm’s Unique Needs

We understand that every farm has its own unique requirements, which is why we offer a fully customizable workforce to meet your specific needs. Whether you require seasonal help for a short-term project or a long-term team to manage ongoing operations, Agribridge provides staffing solutions tailored to your farm’s demands.

Flexible Contract Lengths

We offer staffing solutions that align with the seasonal nature of agricultural work. Whether you need workers for just a few weeks during harvest season or for a full year to assist with long-term projects, we provide flexible contract lengths that fit your needs. Our approach ensures that you can scale your workforce up or down as required, maintaining productivity without unnecessary overheads.

How It Works: Step-by-Step Process for Contractors

At Agribridge, we make the recruitment process simple and efficient. Here’s how we match you with the skilled workers you need:

  1. Submit Your Staffing Needs: Reach out to us with your staffing requirements, detailing the roles you need filled, the number of workers required, and the skills you are looking for.
  2. We Match You with the Right Workers: We carefully match your farm with suitable workers from our pool of experienced candidates, ensuring they have the skills and work ethic needed for your operations.
  3. Contract Finalized: After we’ve found the right workers, we handle all the necessary paperwork, ensuring compliance with legal and employment standards.
  4. Irish Workers Arrive on Your Farm: Once the contract is signed, we assist with travel arrangements and settling your workers into New Zealand, ensuring they are ready to begin work at the start of the season.

By following this straightforward process, we help you quickly and efficiently get the skilled workforce you need for your farm in New Zealand.
