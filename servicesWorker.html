Services for Irish Workers

Your Path to a New Career in New Zealand

Job Opportunities: Explore Agricultural Roles in New Zealand

We offer a wide variety of job opportunities across New Zealand’s vibrant agricultural sector. Whether you’re experienced in livestock care, dairy industry, machinery operation, or general farm labor, we have positions available to suit your skills.

Job Listings: Our website and social media pages feature up-to-date listings with detailed role descriptions, duties, and requirements.

Pay & Benefits: We offer transparent pay structures, working hours, and additional perks such as accommodation allowances and transportation support.

Support During Transition

Bank Account: We assist in setting up a Bank of New Zealand (BNZ) account for smooth financial management during your stay.

Travel Arrangements: We guide you through travel planning, from flight bookings to preparing all necessary documents.

Settling In: We help you adjust to New Zealand life, offering cultural guidance and general support during your arrival.

Visa & Work Permit Assistance

Visa Guidance: We help you with WHV visa applications — from picking the right one to submission.

Ongoing Support: If you encounter issues, our team provides continuous support to ensure a stress-free transition.

By choosing Agribridge, you gain a trusted partner who supports you from job placement to settling into your new life in New Zealand.
