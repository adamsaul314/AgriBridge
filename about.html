How We Operate

We meticulously select experienced farm workers from Ireland, focusing on those who have expertise in key areas such as dairy farming, crop harvesting, and machinery operation.

We facilitate the relocation process, ensuring a smooth transition for both workers and the farms they’ll be joining — guiding them through visas, IRD numbers, bank accounts, accommodation, and cultural adjustments.

At Agribridge Recruitment, we are committed to creating long-term connections between Ireland’s skilled farm workers and New Zealand’s agricultural sector, fostering mutual success and growth.
